Robert Orzanna

“Life is. It does not require any further concretization because any descriptive attempt would narrow down the incomprehensiveness of what life can all be. How does one live a life that is?”1

Hello and welcome. If you want to reach out to me, please best write me an email2. If you want to know more about me, you can take a look at my CV3.

Currently, my main occupation is to support the sustainable development of Sheetgo4. Besides, I endorse to be lazy. There is nothing wrong about it — and it is sustainable, too.

Here is my current list of “I Love To”:

  • Drink apple cider vinegar.
  • Wake up at 05:25 AM.
  • Be boring and go to bed at 10 PM.
  • Check my daily bookmark folder5.
  • Listen to6 and record podcasts7.
  • Improve Ubuntu MATE8.
  • Approve subtitles for the Amara foundation9.
  • Help the FZI10 as a research assistant.
  • Maintain the websites of Research & Degrowth11 and SCORAI12.

Finally, if you are a research scholar, you might be interested in my co-authored publication “Culture meets Flows: The Cultural Dimension of Urban Metabolism”13 that had been cited by the German Advisory Council on Global Change (WBGU) in their report “Humanity on the move: Unlocking the transformative power of cities”14.
