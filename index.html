What are my current life & growth hacks?

I believe you can tweak your life by simple means and grow personally everyday. Here is an excerpt of my tweaks:

  • Differentiating between regular (daily) and occasional consumption. Occasional consumption cannot always be sustainable;

  • Using a simple day structure:

    1. 1.6:00 AM to 09:00 AM: personal development;

    2. 2.09:00 AM to 18:00 PM: professional development;

    3. 3.18:00 PM onward: social development;

  • Doing, repeating and revising things everyday, even if only for a few minutes. Thats the only way to become better and focused at something;

  • Avoiding sugar and salt in my daily life (of course, have exceptions to the rules when being with others)

  • Trying to eat less, smaller amounts, and only when I am actually hungry:

    1. 1.Early morning: coffee or tea;

    2. 2.Late morning: some fruits or juice;

    3. 3.Around midday: lunch;

    4. 4.Afternoon: another small bite;

    5. 5.Evening: dinner;

  • Working at a standing desk;

  • Using earplugs in the city to reduce the noise level especially from cars, buses and lorries to make me feel less stressed and more calm;

  • Sitting on the ground as much as I can with my back straight;

  • Maintaining a long-enough sleep cycle (very challenging for me. Usually conflicts with social development because I live in Spain);

  • Permitting myself to be lazy and not thinking of sleep or napping as a waste of my life time;

  • Making the weekend totally different from the habitual week;

My sustainable consumption and production project:

From research we know that income correlates with consumption which correlates with environmental harm. Or to put it simply: the more you earn, the more likely you are to spend your money on stuff, and hence, the more you are leaving a negative footprint on this planet. This is why I want to understand and make sense of my own consumption patterns. My goal is to minimize my consumption and increase my contribution to the production of things that are in line with my set of values and worldview. I want to invest into the things I truly care about and ignore all the rest. Maintain clear focus. I call this work-in-progress project the Table of Lean & Regular Sustainable Consumption and Production.
